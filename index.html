    What is the Passport Club?


    The Passport Club will be an exclusive alpha group for BBC holders. This community will feature regular talks with influencers and crypto gurus, NFT drops/information, exclusive look and advantages for future BBC drops (airdrop a baby bigfoot to members?), a close-knit community of bigfoot lovers, and much more. The goal of The Passport Club is to continue to add value to holders of the NFT.
